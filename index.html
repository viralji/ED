Monetization: The GST Model

Our monetization strategy is built on a scalable and cohesive model of Goods, Services, and Transformational Experiences (GST), designed to convert users from our free trial into high-value customers.

Core Revenue Streams:

  • Transformational Experiences (Highly Scalable): These are our primary revenue drivers. Our premium subscriptions offer access to AI-guided, multi-week digital journeys (e.g., "40-Day Path to Digital Nirvana"). These leverage our core AI to justify premium pricing and can scale infinitely.
  • Services (Strategically Scalable): We offer services that are built for growth. This includes live virtual workshops and group coaching sessions on specific topics, where a single expert can serve many. We will introduce ultra-premium, 1-to-1 human services in specific geographies, positioning them as an exclusive, high-margin offering.
  • Goods (Digitally Prioritized): We will start with a limited offering of physical goods (e.g., ethically sourced yoga mats, guided journals) to enhance brand identity and then scale this revenue through partnerships to minimize operational overhead. Our primary focus for goods will be on high-margin digital products like premium e-books and advanced audio series.
